About Me

I am the Versatile Audio Alchemist: Weaving Sonic Excellence into Every Experience

As a dedicated Audio and Music Production graduate from Illinois State University, I bring a diverse skill set in live mixing, music production, live music, and studio recording. With experience as an Assistant Stage Manager at ISU's EMDH and as a former Student AV Tech at Edwardsville High School, I've honed my expertise in providing top-notch audio-visual support for a range of events, from intimate gatherings to large-scale concerts.

Driven by a fusion of technical expertise and a creative spirit, I leverage my skills in Logix Pro X, Ableton Live, Pro Tools, and more to curate immersive audio experiences.
